Golden Triangle Tour 6 Days

Delhi - Agra - Fatehpur Sikri - Abhaneri - Jaipur - Delhi

Overview

Golden Triangle Tour 6 days includes Delhi, Agra and Jaipur and the very famous village of Rajasthan called Abhaneri(also known as tank gardens) With the three historic cities, this tour will give you an experience of Indian rural life with Indian food cooking class with dinner where you learn how to cook Indian dishes. Our 5 Nights’ Itinerary helps you enjoy the sights to your heart’s desire and experience the olden glory of India.


Highlights

    • Enjoy rickshaw ride and explore old delhi

    • Visit Qutub Minar, Red Fort, Jama Masjid at Delhi

    • Visit the wonderful Taj Mahal at Agra with tonga ride

    • Visit Fatehpur Sikri enroute to Jaipur

    • Visit Abhaneri(also known as tank gardens) and experience rural life of India

    • Enjoy Indian cooking classes in Jaipur with Indian dinner

    • Explore Amber Fort with elephant ride, City Palace, Hawa Mahal in Jaipur.

Day Wise Itinerary



First day Of 5 days Golden Triangle Tour. You will be Welcomed By Our Rosemont Holidays Company Representative At Arrival On airport and then you will be transferred to hotel. for Overnight stay At Delhi hotel.



Second day of 6 days golden triangle starts with a Sightseeing tour of Delhi meet your guide and at the hotel. Proceed to Monuments – Qutub Minar, Humayun tomb, president house (outside view), Parliament House (outside view), India gate and lotus temple, and after Finishing the tour "Enjoy a rickshaw ride of old Delhi". and Overnight stay at Delhi hotel.



Third day of days 6 Golden Triangle Tour Starts your drive towards to city of Taj Agra via the Yamuna Expressway (3 hours journey). Visit Itmad-Ud-Daulah Tomb (Baby Taj). and "Enjoy your tonga ride" and visit Agra fort. Then check into the Hotel and rest. Overnight at Agra city.



Fourth day of days 6 golden triangle tour start your Guiding tour taj mahal city Agra. Meet your chauffeur and guide at the hotel Early morning sunrise tour of taj mahal. proceed to visit taj mahal at sunrise Time then come back to the hotel For having a healthy breakfast and check out. After grabbing the luggage From hotel and to proceed to fatehpur sikri, made as a tribute to a sufi Saint sheik salim chisti visit fatehpur sikri then drive to the small Village of abhaneri has one of india's deepest and largest step wells (also known as tank gardens). Step wells are unique to india. They were Used as cool places of resort, as pools for ritual cleansing before a Temple visit and as a water supply for dry weather. There is a temple Adjoining the stepwell. And drive to jaipur city, the city of colors And also known as the pink city. Stay overnight in Jaipur.



Fifth day of the 6 Days Golden Triangle Tour After breakfast start for the breathtaking tour of the pink city Jaipur. Meet your chauffeur and guide at the hotel and go for a sightseeing tour for the fort of Amber where you will be having the experience of an elephant ride. Later visit the city palace, the observatory (Jantar Mantar). Take a photo stop at Jal Mahal (water palace) and Hawa Mahal (wind palace) and visit Birla Temple. Jaipur is also known for Jewelry and textile. after the city sightseeing go back to your hotel and take some rest then your chauffeur will come to pick you for your cooking class where you learn about some Indian dishes and enjoy your dinner also after cooking then come to back to your hotel stay overnight in Jaipur.



Sixth and departure day of the 6 days golden triangle .After breakfast or as per your flight schedule, pick up from the hotel and Drive to delhi, transfer to delhi airport to catch flight for onward destination with sweet memories of the tour.
