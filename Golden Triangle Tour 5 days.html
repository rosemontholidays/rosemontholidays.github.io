Golden Triangle Tour 5 Days

Delhi - Agra - Fatehpur Sikri - Jaipur - Delhi

Overview

Golden Triangle Tour 5 days includes Delhi, Agra and Jaipur. With the three historic cities, this tour will give you an experience of Indian food cooking class with dinner where you learn how to cook Indian dishes about. Through 4 nights 5 Days Golden Triangle Tour you will be exploring the three most exotic cities of India Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur with Indian cooking classes and take a ride to the most amazing ride in history.


Highlights

  • Enjoy rickshaw ride and explore old delhi

  • Visit Qutub Minar, Red Fort, Jama Masjid at Delhi

  • Visit the wonderful Taj Mahal at Agra with tonga ride

  • Visit Fatehpur Sikri enroute to Jaipur

  • Enjoy Indian cooking classes in Jaipur with Indian dinner

  • Explore Amber Fort with elephant ride, City Palace, Hawa Mahal in Jaipur.

Day Wise Itinerary



First day of 5 days golden triangle tour. You will be welcomed by our rosemont holidays company representative at arrival on airport and then you will be transferred to hotel take some rest then go for rickshaw ride to old Delhi explore old Delhi then come back to your hotel and overnight stay at Delhi hotel.



Second day of 5 Days Golden Triangle Starts with a sightseeing tour of Old and New Delhi En your Meet your guide and at the hotel. Proceed to monuments – Qutub Minar, Humayun Tomb, President House (outside view), Parliament House (outside view), India Gate and Lotus Temple, After finishing the tour proceed for the city of love - Agra, via the Yamuna Expressway (3 hours journey). After arriving in Agra check into the Hotel and rest. Overnight at Agra city.



Third day of 5 days golden triangle tour start your guiding tour of the city of Taj Mahal city Agra. Meet your chauffeur and guide at hotel early morning sunrise tour of Taj Mahal .proceed to visit Taj Mahal at sunrise time then come back to hotel for having healthy breakfast and check out. After grabbing the luggage from hotel "Enjoy your tonga ride" and proceed to agra fort build in the mid of the 15th century.Then proceed to Fatehpur Sikri, made as a tribute to a sufi saint sheik salim chisti. Drive to jaipur city, the city of colours and also known as pink city. Stay overnight in Jaipur.



Fourth day of 5 days golden triangle tour after breakfast starts for the breathtaking tour of the pink city Jaipur. Meet your chauffeur and guide at the hotel and go for a sightseeing tour for the fort of Amber where you will be having the experience of an elephant ride. Later visit the city palace, the observatory (Jantar Mantar). Take a photo stop at Jal Mahal (water palace) and Hawa Mahal (wind palace) and visit Birla temple. Jaipur is also known for Jewelry and textile. After the city sightseeing goes back to your hotel and take some rest then your chauffeur will come to pick you for your cooking class where you learn about some Indian dishes and enjoy your dinner also after cooking then come to back to your hotel stay overnight in Jaipur.



Fifth and departure day of 5 days golden triangle. After breakfast or as per your flight schedule, pick up from hotel and drive to Delhi, transfer to Delhi airport to catch flight for onward destination with sweet memories of tour.
