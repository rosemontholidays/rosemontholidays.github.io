The Majestic Rajasthan Tour

Delhi - Mandawa - Bikaner - Jaisalmer - Jodhpur - Ranakpur - Udaipur - Pushkar - Jaipur - Fatehpur Sikri - Agra - Delhi

Overview

The Majestic Rajasthan tour begins Delhi and ends in Delhi the Indian capital.The Destination covered in this tour are Mandawa famous for havelis, Bikaner for Junagadh fort , Jaisalmer famous for the romantic desert , Jodhpur the "Blue city" of Rajasthan, Next is romantic city Udaipur dotted with lakes enroute to udaipur, we will visit Ranakpur Jain Temple, then we proceed to Pink City Jaipur, and the last destination is the city of love and Taj Mahal Agra enroute to Agra we will visit Fatehpur Sikri .


Highlights

  • Enjoy rickshaw ride and explore old delhi

  • Visit Qutub Minar, Red Fort, Jama Masjid at Delhi

  • Visit the wonderful Taj Mahal at Agra with tonga ride

  • Mohabbat the Taj Show in Agra

  • Visit Fatehpur Sikri enroute to Agra

  • Visit Abhaneri(also known as tank gardens) and experience rural life of India

  • Enjoy Indian cooking classes in Jaipur with Indian dinner

  • Explore Amber Fort with elephant ride, City Palace, Hawa Mahal in Jaipur.

  • Explore Mehrangarh Fort in Jodhpur

  • Explore Bishnoi village with jeep safari

  • Visit Brahma ji temple and Ranakpur Jain temple

  • Enjoy lake pichola boat ride in Udaipur

  • Enjoy the camel ride at Thar Desert and Enjoy Camping with folk dance in jaisalmer

  • Explore the Popular Hawelis of Mandawa

Day Wise Itinerary



First day of Majestic Rajasthan tour. You will be Greet by our Rosemont holidays representative at Delhi airport, our Representative will escort you to the hotel freshen up the hotel. Stay overnight in Delhi.



Second day of the majestic Rajasthan tour Start your Guiding tour of a sightseeing tour of Delhi meet your guide and chauffeur at the hotel. Proceed to monuments – Qutub Minar, Humayun tomb, president house (outside view), parliament house (outside view), India gate and lotus temple, and "Enjoy your old Delhi rickshaw ride" then come back to your hotel for an overnight stay.



Third day of majestic Rajasthan tour. After breakfast check drives towards Mandawa arrive Mandawa check-in to the hotel and take some rest and start your guiding tour of Mandawa known as ‘open art gallery due to a large number of mansions (Havelis) beautified by fresco paintings depicting mythological and historical themes. Famous Havelis are Gulab bai Wadia haveli, Ganeriwala haveli, Jhunjhunwala haveli, Kaneria haveli, Goenka haveli, and many more. Then return back to your prebooked hotel for an overnight stay in mandawa.



Fourth day of majestic Rajasthan tour after breakfast drive to Bikaner. Arrive Bikaner get relaxed for a while and proceed along with Bikaner tour guide to visit famous monuments and tourist attractions of Bikaner which primarily include magnificent Junagarh fort, splendid lalgarh palace, and the camel breeding farm & research center. Spend rest of the day at leisure and have night stay in Bikaner hotel



Fifth day of majestic Rajasthan tour after breakfast depart for Jaisalmer,arrive at Jaisalmer check in to your hotel relax for some time and in evening visit "Vyas chhatri sunset point" In Jaisalmer and stay overnight in your prebooked hotel in Jaisalmer.



Sixth day of majestic Rajasthan tour after breakfast in the jaisalmer hotel, move on for visiting the jaisalmer fort, popularly known as “sonar qila (golden fort)”. Then visit prominent Havelis like Nathmalji-ki-haveli & Patwon -ki-haveli along with Manak chowk. An excursion to sam dunes will take place. The excursion will be a camel ride/ safari in the sand dunes of Thar desert. The camel safari, night-stay in tented accommodation in the thar desert, and dinner on sand dunes while folk dance and music being performed will be incredible aspects of real Rajasthan. And stay overnight at a desert camp in Jaisalmer.



Seventh day of majestic Rajasthan tour proceed desert camp and after breakfast departs for Jodhpur, arrive Jodhpur check in to your hotel take some rest then you will be taken to craftsmen villages of Bishnoi tribe. Having enjoyed a delicious breakfast in Jodhpur hotel, move on an excursion to Bishnoi village to have a look at real rural life in a Rajasthani village and villagers’ skill sets and value systems. Enjoy mingling with rural folks, participate in religious rituals & experience real Rajasthan. Then come back to your hotel stay overnight in Jodhpur.



Eight day of majestic Rajasthan tour after breakfast, guided sightseeing tour of Jodhpur city will commence with a visit to the Mehrangarh fort. It is a fortified military fortress with beautiful palaces, temples, and gardens inside. Mehrangarh is a fort that has always resisted the attacks, without ever being violated in its five hundred years of history. Continue your tour with a visit to Jaswant Thada, located a short distance from the fort, which is a white marble monument for maharaja Jaswant Singh ii. In the afternoon, explore the local market, abuzz with activity. Later in the evening, return to the hotel to spend the evening in leisure and have an overnight stay.



Ninth day of majestic Rajasthan tour after breakfast, depart for Udaipur. On the way to ‘the city of lakes, stop at Ranakpur, a Jain pilgrimage. Ranakpur is famous for its Jain temples complex. Built in the fifteenth century entirely in white marble, it will proceed you delighted by the sophistication of inlays and workmanship. Post lunch, continue to drive to Udaipur. On arrival in Udaipur, check-in at the hotel for an overnight stay.



Tenth day of the majestic Rajasthan tour. After breakfast, this day, you will wander through the markets and bazaars to visit the Jagdish temple and then continue to explore the romantic city from the city palace, which is regarded as the largest of its type in Rajasthan. Later take a relaxing boat ride on Lake Pichola, before heading for the garden of the maids of honor and folk art museum. After that, you will enjoy an unforgettable sunset by visiting the sajjan garh palace (monsoon palace). Later come back to the hotel udaipur and overnight stay.



Eleventh day of the majestic Rajasthan tour after breakfast, depart Udaipur and drive towards the pink city Jaipur en-route Jaipur stop at Pushkar, a Hindu pilgrimage. Pushkar, popular for the Brahma temple offers the opportunity to observe rituals and traditions of devout Hindus. Arrive at Jaipur stay overnight at a hotel in Jaipur.



Twelfth day of the majestic rajasthan tour start for the breathtaking tour of the pink city jaipur. Meet your chauffeur and guide at the hotel and go for a sightseeing tour for the fort of amber where you will be having the experience of an elephant ride. Later visit the city palace, the observatory (jantar mantar). Take a photo stop at jal mahal (water palace) and hawa Mahal (wind palace) and visit birla temple. Jaipur is also known for Jewelry and textile. After the city sightseeing go back to your hotel and take some rest then your chauffeur will come to pick you for your cooking class where you learn about some indian dishes and enjoy your dinner also after cooking then come to back to your hotel stay overnight in Jaipur



Thirteenth day of majestic Rajasthan tour after breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to the city of love and taj mahal Agra. Having a healthy breakfast and check out. After grabbing the luggage from the hotel proceed to the small village of Abha Neri has one of India's deepest and largest step wells (also known as tank gardens). Step wells are unique to India. They were used as cool places of resort, as pools for ritual cleansing before a temple visit, and as a water supply for dry weather. There is a temple adjoining the stepwell. And to proceed to Fatehpur Sikri, made as a tribute to a Sufi saint sheik Salim Chisti visit Fatehpur Sikri then drive to Agra arrive Agra check in to hotel stay overnight in city.



Fourteenth day of majestic Rajasthan tour after breakfast,start your guiding tour of the city of taj mahal city Agra. Meet your chauffeur and guide at the hotel early morning sunrise tour of Taj mahal.proceed to visit taj mahal at sunrise time then come back to the hotel for having a healthy breakfast "Enjoy your tonga ride" and proceed to Agra Fort build in the mid of the 15th century. And visit itmad-ud-daulah tomb (baby taj) then come back to your prebooked hotel stay overnight in Agra .enjoy Mohabbat the taj show that depicts the romantic love story of Emperor Shahjahan and his beloved wife Mumtaz mahal you will be blown away by the creative dance and dramatic lighting with the lavish lifestyle of the Mughal empire. Then come back to your hotel stay overnight in Agra.



Fifteenth day and departure day of majestic Rajasthan tour after breakfast you will be transferred to Delhi from Delhi airport you can take flight for your onward destination. And your tour ends with sweet memories.
