Taj Mahal tour with Chambal

Delhi - Chambal - Bateshwar - Agra - Delhi

Overview

Experience your Journey of Taj Mahal with The Chambal National Sanctuary, bordering the Chambal river in Central India, is home to several highly endangered species of birds and animals, including the Gharial, Gangetic Dolphins, Marsh Crocodiles (Muggers), several species of Turtles, Smooth Coated Otters, Indian Striped Hyenas etc. and Bateshwar village in Agra District, on the banks of the river Yamuna in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh, India. Bateshwar is an important spiritual and cultural centre for Hindus and Jains. It is known for the 101 Shiv Temple Complex.


Highlights

  • Visit the monument of love Taj Mahal during sunrise

  • Visit Agra Fort build in mid of 15th century

  • Visit Itmad-Ud-Daulah Tomb (Baby Taj)

  • Enjoy tonga ride in Agra

  • Visit Chambal National Sanctuary

  • River safari Chambal National Sanctuary

  • Visit Bateshwar cultural centre for Hindus and Jains known for the 101 Shiv Temple complex.

Itinerary



First day of Taj mahal tour with Chambal safari our Rosemont holidays representative will come to pick you from the hotel/airport in Delhi. And take you to Chambal national sanctuary, via the Yamuna expressway (3 hours journey). After arriving check into the Check-in to the Chambal safari lodge. Bordering one of central India's longest rivers the Chambal national sanctuary is unique in its flora, fauna, and birdlife. It is one of the last surviving habitats of the Gangetic dolphins and is home to a large number of gharials and marsh crocodiles. Where you have the opportunity to see dolphins, gharials, crocodiles, turtles, and almost all the other species of mammals and birds that the Chambal national sanctuary is famous for. In the afternoon you will enjoy, river safari into the national Chambal sanctuary with excellent naturalists are at hand to enhance the experience. Then you will visit the nearby Shiva temples in Bateshwar, where there are almost 30 temples picturesquely located on the banks of the river Yamuna. Overnight stay in Chambal.



The second and departure day of Taj mahal tour with the Chambal safari tour start your Guiding tour of taj mahal. Meet your chauffeur who will take you to Agra early morning sunrise tour of Taj Mahal. Visit taj mahal at sunrise time then got to the luxury restaurant for having a healthy breakfast. After your breakfast " enjoy tonga ride" and proceed to agra fort built in mid of 15 century after visiting our assistance (driver) will drop you we will proceed for Delhi, we will drop you at airport/hotel as per your Convenience
