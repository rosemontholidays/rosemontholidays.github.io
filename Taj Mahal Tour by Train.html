Taj Mahal Tour by Train

Delhi - Agra - Delhi

Overview

Experience your Journey to Taj Mahal by Superfast Train Gatimaan Express. This tour is generally for those travelers who look for comfort and want to save their time because Gatimaan Express is a Superfast train that covers Delhi to Agra journey in 100 minutes so this same-day Taj Mahal tour by train from Delhi is a perfect tour for travelers who want to discover Taj Mahal in one day


Highlights

  • Enjoy luxury train Gatimaan Express journey with breakfast and dinner

  • Visit the monument of love Taj Mahal

  • Visit Agra Fort build in mid of 15th century

  • Indian buffet lunch in five star hotel

  • Enjoy tonga ride in Agra

  • Visit local Mughal which is from the time of Mughal Emperor Shahjahan

Itinerary



You will be picked up from your desired location be it anywhere at a hotel, airport, or railway station in Delhi/NCR/Gurgaon via Car and drop you at Nizamuddin railway station start your tour with a healthy breakfast on the train. You will be provided with e-tickets either by email or through our representative. The train starts at 8 am and reaches Agra at 9:45 am.



Around 10:15 am meet your tour guide and you will be at The premises of the taj mahal. It is the main attraction of the city and Not to miss sight for all visitors to India. It is great and the most Phenomenal structure in the world and is seen as an image of love and Affection. The 17th-century monument of love, built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in the memory of his wife Mumtaz mahal. Taj Mahal remain Closed on Friday.



After visit taj mahal "Enjoy your tonga ride" to Agra fort. Soon enough you are the agra fort which also goes by the Names of “lal- qila” and “qila-I-Akbari”, is a symbol of power, Fortitude and grandeur. One simply cannot afford to miss the Agra fort when in the historical city of Agra. Agra fort tour takes you into the The historical era of the Mughals and their royal lifestyle, offering great Views of the overwhelming facades and mighty minarets. Enjoy your guided Agra Fort tour.



After exploring the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort, we will move on to a five-star hotel where you get to enjoy the real taste of Indian buffet lunch.



Later on, after eating a delicious breakfast, we proceed for craft art, textiles, handicrafts, and demonstration of marble inlay work in Agra that is a rich legacy of the craftsmanship of the artists at Agra. Take home unique handicraft items and lots of souvenirs from the shops to remember your exciting time in agra. This art is from the time of Mughal emperor Shahjahan which will take you into the Mughal era of history.



After exploring Taj Mahal, Agra fort, lunch, and the local Art of agra our assistant (driver) will drop you at agra cantt railway station to catch gatimaan express for delhi enjoy your dinner in train. Our driver will be waiting for you at Nizamuddin railway station and he will drop you at the airport/hotel as per your Convenience.
