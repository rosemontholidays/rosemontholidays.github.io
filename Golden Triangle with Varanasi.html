Golden Triangle with Varanasi

Delhi - Jaipur - Abhaneri - FatehpurSikri - Agra - Varanasi - Delhi

Overview

Golden Triangle Tour with Varanasi includes India's 4 major city Delhi, Agra, Jaipur and the city of holy river ganges Varanasi this tour will give you an experience of historical and spiritual India where you enjoy boat ride in Varanasi and visit Sarnath â€“ a religious Buddhist site and many more then in evening you will visit the banks of "Ganges for Ganga Aarti."


Highlights

  • Visit Qutub Minar, Red Fort, Jama Masjid at Delhi

  • Enjoy Old Delhi rickshaw ride

  • Visit the wonderful Taj Mahal at Agra with tonga ride

  • Visit Fatehpur Sikri enroute to Jaipur

Day Wise Itinerary



First day of golden triangle tour with Varanasi. You will be Greet by our Rosemont holidays representative at Delhi airport, our representative will escort you to the hotel freshen up the hotel. And take some rest then enjoy your rickshaw ride of old Delhi then come back to your hotel stay overnight in Delhi



Second day of golden triangle tour with Varanasi start your Guiding tour of a sightseeing tour of Delhi meet your guide and chauffeur at the hotel. Proceed to monuments â€“ Qutub Minar, Humayun tomb, president house (outside view), parliament house (outside view), India gate and lotus temple, and after finishing proceed to the pink city. After arriving Jaipur check into the Hotel and rest overnight at Jaipur city.



Third day of golden triangle tour with Varanasi. Start your guiding tour of the city of taj mahal city Agra. Meet your chauffeur and guide at the hotel early morning sunrise tour of the taj mahal. proceed to visit taj mahal at a sunrise time then come back to the hotel for having a healthy breakfast and check out. After grabbing the luggage from hotel "Enjoy your tonga ride" and proceed to Agra Fort build in the mid of the 15th century. Then proceed to Fatehpur Sikri, made as a tribute to a Sufi saint Sheik Salim Chisti. Drive to Jaipur city, the city of colors and also known as the pink city. Stay overnight in Jaipur.



Fourth day of the golden triangle tour with Varanasi after Grabbing the luggage from hotel and proceeding to the small village of Abhaneri has one of India's deepest and largest step wells (also known as Tank gardens). Step wells are unique to India. They were used as cool places Of resort, as pools for ritual cleansing before a temple visit and as a Water supply for dry weather. There is a temple adjoining the step well then Continue your drive to Agra en-route visit Fatehpur Sikri, made as a tribute To a sufi saint sheik salim chisti visit fatehpur sikri then drive to agra City, the city of love and taj mahal stay overnight in Agra.



Fifth day of golden triangle tour with Varanasi start your Guiding tour of the city of taj mahal city Agra. Meet your chauffeur and Guide at the hotel early morning sunrise tour of taj mahal. proceed to visit taj mahal at a sunrise time then come back To hotel for having healthy breakfast take some rest and check out. After grabbing the Luggage from hotel "Enjoy your tonga ride" and proceed to agra Fort builds in the mid of 15th century. Then proceed to visit itmad-ud-daulah tomb (baby taj).And catch your train to varanasi in the evening stay overnight on the train.



Sixth day of golden triangle tour with varanasi receives a warm welcome from our tour representative who will help you with a comfortable transfer to the hotel. Check-in and unwind yourself. Subsequently, proceed for an excursion to sarnath. Nestled near the confluence of river ganga and varuna, sarnath is one of the four holy buddhist sites. A half-day trip to varanasi is scheduled for rest of the day. The destinations which will be covered are the Alamgir Mosque or Beni Madhav ka dare, dashashwamedh ghat, and the Bharat Mata temple. Built-in the 17th-century by Aurangzeb, the mosque portrays a perfect blend of the Hindu and Islamic architecture. Dashashwamedh ghat enjoys the worldwide repute for hosting the evening ganga aarti. Rather than housing statues of gods and goddesses, the bharat mata temple is home to a huge map of india carved in marble. "Attend the evening ganga aarti in varanasi and boat ride" then return to the hotel for an overnight stay



Seventh and departure day of golden triangle tour with varanasi after breakfast, transfer to the airport for your flight to Delhi. Once you arrive at Delhi airport, you can take your flight for your onward journey
