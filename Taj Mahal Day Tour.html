Taj Mahal Tours

A trip to India is not complete if you do not visit the "Taj Mahal". And this is not an over-the-top statement. Famous as a symbol of love, this magnificent monument has been making onlookers turn green with envy for ages. In short, regardless of whether you are from this country or from overseas, one trip to Agra and feasting your eyes on the "Taj Mahal" is a must, at least once in your lifetime. Our overnight"Taj Mahal" tour packages contains many speical attractions and activities which are oppurtunity to visit the "Taj Mahal" , "Agra Fort" , "Baby - Taj", "Bateshwar", village in Agra District, on the banks of the river Yamuna in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh, India. Bateshwar is an important spiritual and cultural centre for Hindus and Jains. It is known for the 101 Shiv Temple Complex. and "The Mohabbat The Taj Show" (Kalakriti Show) that depicts the romantic love story of Emperor Shajahan and his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal in the famous theater Kalakriti Cultural and Convention Center. You will be blown away by the creative dance and dramatic lighting with the 12’ x 12’ Taj Mahal replica as well as the lavish lifestyle of the Mughal Empire. The live show is available in English, French , German and spanish.Check our Most Popular "Taj Mahal" Tour Packages To Create Memories with "Taj Mahal" These Packages are for those people who want to discover the "Taj Mahal" in one-two days.
