Golden Triangle With Amritsar

Delhi - Agra - FatehpurSikri - Jaipur - Amritsar - Delhi

Overview

Golden Triangle Tour with Udaipur includes India's 4 major city Delhi, Agra, Jaipur and the the tour will take you to the sacred city Amritsar where you can visit the Golden Temple, the ‘Mecca of Sikhs’ and other Sikh pilgrimage places. Pay a tribute to the innocent victims of the Jallianwala Bagh massacre. Spend an evening at the Wagah Border to witness the flag retreat between two neighboring countries India and Pakistan.


Highlights

  • Visit Qutub Minar, Red Fort, Jama Masjid at Delhi

  • Enjoy Old Delhi rickshaw ride

  • Visit the wonderful Taj Mahal at Agra with tonga ride

  • Visit Fatehpur Sikri enroute to Jaipur

  • Explore Amber Fort with elephant ride, City Palace, Hawa Mahal in Jaipur.

  • Enjoy Indian cooking classes in Jaipur with Indian dinner

  • Visit Attari Border (Wagah Border of Pakistan)where every evening a salute exchange ceremony hosted by Indian Army.

  • visit to the magnificent premises of Golden Temple.

Day Wise Itinerary



First day of golden triangle tour with Amritsar. You will be Greet by our Rosemont holidays representative at Delhi airport, our representative will escort you to the hotel freshen up in the hotel. And take some rest. Then enjoy your rickshaw ride of old Delhi then come back to your hotel stay overnight in Delhi



Second day of Golden Triangle Tour with Amritsar Start your Guiding tour of a sightseeing tour of Delhi Meets your guide and chauffeur at the hotel. Proceed to monuments – Qutub Minar, Humayun Tomb, President House (outside view), Parliament House (outside view), India Gate and Lotus Temple, and After finishing proceed to the city of love - Agra, via the Yamuna Expressway (3 hours journey). After arriving in Agra check into the Hotel and rest Overnight at Agra city.



Third day of the golden triangle tour with Amritsar. Start your guiding tour of the city of Taj Mahal city Agra. Meet your chauffeur and guide at the hotel early morning sunrise tour of the Taj Mahal. proceed to visit taj mahal at a sunrise time then come back to the hotel for having a healthy breakfast and check out. After grabbing the luggage from hotel "Enjoy your tonga ride" and proceed to Agra fort build in the mid of the 15th century. Then proceed to Fatehpur Sikri, made as a tribute to a Sufi saint Sheik Salim Chisti. Drive to Jaipur city, the city of colors and also known as the pink city. Stay overnight in Jaipur



Fourth day of golden triangle tour with Amritsar after breakfast starts for the breathtaking tour of the pink city Jaipur. Meet your chauffeur and guide at the hotel and go for a sightseeing tour for the fort of Amber where you will be having the experience of an elephant ride. Later visit the city palace, the observatory (Jantar Mantar). Take a photo stop at Jal Mahal (water palace) and Hawa Mahal (wind palace) and visit Birla temple. Jaipur is also known for Jewelry and textile. After the city sightseeing go back to your hotel and take some rest then your chauffeur will come to pick you for your cooking class where you learn about some Indian dishes and enjoy your dinner also after cooking then come to back to your hotel stay overnight in Jaipur



Fifth day of the golden triangle tour with Amritsar after breakfast and check-out from Jaipur hotel and drive to Delhi. From Delhi railway station to catch your prebooked luxury train Shatabdi express. When you arrive in Amritsar, our representative will meet you at the railway station he will take you to your prebooked hotel. Check-in to your hotel and stay overnight in Amritsar.



Sixth day of Golden Triangle Tour with Amritsar The morning at the pious city of Amritsar will begin with the early morning visit to the magnificent premises of Golden Temple. After the temple visit enjoys local food and embellished market fully flourished around the Golden Temple. After lunch, it is worth to visit Attari Border (Wagah Border of Pakistan) situated at National Highway 1 where every evening a salute exchange ceremony is hosted by the Indian Army. The enthusiasm and charms amongst the Indian visitors will make you feel like a part of it. Stay overnight in Amritsar.



Seventh and Departure day of Golden Triangle Tour with Amritsar After breakfast, it is time for another train ride to reach Delhi. When you arrive in Delhi, our representative will meet you at Delhi Railway Station and he will drop you at Airport in Delhi So You can take your flight for your onward journey.
