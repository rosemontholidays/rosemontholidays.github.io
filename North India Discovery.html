North India Discovery Tour

Delhi - Jaipur - Abhaneri - Fatehpur Sikri - Agra - Gwalior - orchha - khajuraho - Varanasi - Delhi

Overview

The Colors of North India tour begins and ends in Delhi, the Indian capital.Delhi,the city of love and Taj Mahal Agra the pink city Jaipur along with the land of holy river ganges Varanasi and exotic Khajuraho. Some of the highlights include exploring mighty forts of Pink city Jaipur, boat ride on River Ganges in Varanasi, visiting UNESCO World Sites of Khajuraho temples where you enjoy the folk dance show also etc., shopping at the local bazaars and much more.


Highlights

  • Enjoy rickshaw ride and explore old delhi

  • Visit Qutub Minar, Red Fort, Jama Masjid at Delhi

  • Visit the wonderful Taj Mahal at Agra with tonga ride

  • Mohabbat the Taj Show in Agra

  • Visit Fatehpur Sikri enroute to Agra

  • Visit Abhaneri(also known as tank gardens) and experience rural life of India

  • Enjoy Indian cooking classes in Jaipur with Indian dinner

  • Explore Amber Fort with elephant ride, City Palace, Hawa Mahal in Jaipur.

  • Visit the royal Gwalior Fort

  • Explore temples of Orchha and exotic Khajuraho

  • Enjoy boat ride and Ganga Arti in Varanasi

Day Wise Itinerary



First day of north India discovery tour. You will be Greet by our Rosemont holidays representative at Delhi airport, our representative will escort you to the hotel freshen up in the hotel And take some rest. Then enjoy your rickshaw ride of old Delhi then come back to your hotel stay overnight in Delhi



Second day of the north India discovery tour start your Guiding tour of a sightseeing tour of Delhi meets your guide and chauffeur at the hotel. Proceed to monuments – Qutub Minar, Humayun tomb, president house (outside view), parliament house (outside view), India gate and lotus temple, and after finishing left to the pink city - Jaipur, after arriving Jaipur check into the Hotel and rest overnight at Jaipur city.



Third day of the north India discovery tour after breakfast starts for the breathtaking tour of the pink city Jaipur. Meet your chauffeur and guide at the hotel and go for a sightseeing tour for the fort of Amber where you will be having the experience of an elephant ride. Later visit the city palace, the observatory (Jantar Mantar). Take a photo stop at Jal Mahal (water palace) and Hawa Mahal (wind palace) and visit Birla temple. Jaipur is also known for Jewelry and textile. After the city sightseeing go back to your hotel and take some rest then your chauffeur will come to pick you for your cooking class where you learn about some Indian dishes and enjoy your dinner also after cooking then come to back to your hotel stay overnight at Jaipur



Fourth day of the north India discovery tour having a healthy breakfast and check out. After grabbing the luggage from the hotel proceed to the small village of Abha Neri has one of India's deepest and largest step wells (also known as tank gardens). Step wells are unique to india. They were used as cool places of resort, as pools for ritual cleansing before a temple visit, and as a water supply for dry weather. There is a temple adjoining the stepwell. And to proceed to Fatehpur Sikri, made as a tribute to a Sufi saint sheik Salim Chisti visit Fatehpur Sikri then drive to Agra arrive Agra check in to the hotel and take some rest then go to enjoy Mohabbat the taj show that depicts the romantic love story of Emperor Shahjahan and his beloved wife Mumtaz mahal you will be blown away by the creative dance and dramatic lighting and lavish lifestyle of the Mughal empire. Then come back to your hotel stay overnight at Agra.



Fifth day of north India discovery tour start your guiding tour of the city of Taj mahal city Agra. Meet your chauffeur and guide at the hotel early morning sunrise tour of the taj mahal is the best time to visit the taj mahal. proceed to visit taj mahal at sunrise time then come back to the hotel for having a healthy breakfast. And check out. After grabbing the luggage from hotel "Enjoy your tonga ride" and proceed to Agra fort build in the mid of the 15th century. Then proceed to Gwalior after arriving Gwalior check into the Hotel and rest overnight at Gwalior city.



Sixth day of the North India discovery tour after breakfast we will continue the day visiting the gwalior fort and the jai vilas palace then continue your drive to Orchha in the afternoon we will visit the historical monuments of orchha. The grandeur of ancient orchha was captured in the stone of its monuments and frozen over the centuries. In this medieval city rich in palaces and temples built by the bundela rulers in the 16th and 17th centuries, much of their works have been preserved. Jehangir mahal - a multi-tiered palace crowned by the graceful chhatries has a spectacular view of the temples, pyres, and cenotaphs. And stay overnight at hotel in Orchha.



Seventh day north India discovery tour after breakfast departure for Khajuraho, famous for its erotic wall sculptures. We will visit the temples of Khajuraho which were built by the kings of Chandela between 250 ad and 1050 ad The most important is the chainset yogini temple dedicated to the goddess Kali, the Mahadev temple, the Chitragupta or Bharathi temple with a beautiful image of 11 vishnu, vishwanath and nandi temples, Lakshmana temple, visha temple dedicated to Shiva, which is the largest and most characteristic of india. Stay overnight at the hotel in Khajuraho and enjoy the folk dance show in khajuraho and stay overnight at the hotel in Khajuraho.



Eight day after breakfast get an assisted transfer to the airport to board a flight to Varanasi. On arrival, meet your representative who offers you an assisted transfer to the hotel. The evening is at leisure. Stay overnight at the hotel in Varanasi



Ninth day of north India discovery tour after the early morning during sunrise our driver and guide will come to receive you at the hotel and move you to the river ganges. Enjoy the sunrise view on river ganges and a boat ride. come back to the hotel and after breakfast continue your sightseeing. Visit sarnath – a religious buddhist site, alamgir mosque, Beni Madhav ka darera, Dasaswamedh Ghat, and the Bharat Mata temple. Later come back to the hotel and in the evening you will visit the banks of "The Ganges for Ganga aarti." overnight stay Varanasi in the hotel.



Tenth and departure day of north India discovery tour after breakfast, after breakfast, you will be transferred in time to Varanasi airport to board a flight for Delhi airport from Delhi you can take a flight for your onward destination.
